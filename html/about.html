About Us!

Mission:

Here at Tempera we've created a website to cater to artists and art lovers.
As an artist, you will be able to have your own store to reach an audience of eager art patrons.
YOU are in charge of all delivery and transaction of your art. YOU have complete control over your art; we exist to help you get noticed!
As an art patron, you will be able to view any stores on our website created by artists.
In each store, an artist will create listings in hopes of catching your eye!
If you are interested in obtaining the artwork, send them a message through the listing page!

Designers: Andrew and Hannah

Hello! We are a web developing duo!
Hannah specialized in the frontend as Andrew specialized in the backend.
We've meshed our skillsets in order to provide the art community with an easy way to obtain and sell art.
At San Diego State University, we noticed many artists didn't have the best platform to get their work noticed.
We hope this changes that!
